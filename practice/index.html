Areas of practice.

A litigation and dispute-resolution practice with an integrated bench of counsel admitted before the Supreme Court of India, the High Court of Delhi and specialised tribunals including the NCLT, NCLAT, NCDRC, DRT and DRAT.

01

Commercial Litigation

The firm acts for Indian and multinational corporations, financial institutions and senior individuals in complex commercial and civil disputes before the Supreme Court of India, the High Courts and the district courts. Our partners and associates appear regularly across India and have done so for over three decades.

  • Contract and shareholder disputes
  • Banking and recovery litigation
  • Corporate fraud and white-collar defence
  • Civil suits, writ petitions and appeals
  • Injunctions and interim relief
  • Enforcement proceedings
Lead Partner Amarjit Singh Bedi
02

Arbitration

We act as counsel in domestic and international arbitration proceedings, including matters administered by the Delhi International Arbitration Centre. The team has experience across institutional rules and ad-hoc references, in both pre-reference applications and post-award challenges.

  • Domestic institutional and ad-hoc arbitration
  • International commercial arbitration
  • Section 9 and Section 17 interim relief
  • Award challenges and enforcement
  • Multi-party and complex commercial references
  • Pre-arbitration negotiation and conciliation
Lead Associate Sidharth Nair
03

Insolvency & Restructuring

The firm represents financial creditors, operational creditors, resolution applicants and corporate debtors in proceedings under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, 2016. We appear regularly before the NCLT, NCLAT, DRT and DRAT in both contested and resolution matters.

  • Section 7, 9 and 10 applications
  • Corporate insolvency resolution process (CIRP)
  • Resolution-applicant representation
  • Liquidation proceedings
  • Avoidance applications and recovery
  • Appellate work before the NCLAT
Lead Partner Amarjit Singh Bedi
04

Securities & Regulatory

We advise and represent clients in proceedings before the Securities and Exchange Board of India and related forums. Recent matters have included representation in market-conduct proceedings of national importance, including SEBI investigations and appeals.

  • SEBI investigations and proceedings
  • Insider-trading and disclosure matters
  • Market-manipulation defence
  • Securities Appellate Tribunal appearances
  • Listing-obligation compliance counsel
  • Capital-markets regulatory advisory
Lead Partner Amarjit Singh Bedi
05

Real Estate & RERA

The firm acts for developers, investors and home-buyers in real-estate matters under the Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, including RERA complaints and appeals. Our team also prepares due-diligence reports and handles title work and real-estate disputes across Delhi-NCR.

  • RERA complaints and appeals
  • Title due diligence and reports
  • Real-estate dispute resolution
  • Builder–buyer matters
  • Land-acquisition disputes
  • Conveyancing and lease counsel
Lead Advocate Harshit Singh
06

Corporate Advisory

The firm provides strategic advisory to senior management of Indian and international clients on contentious and non-contentious corporate matters, contract vetting, due diligence and corporate-governance counsel.

  • Strategic management counsel
  • Contract drafting and vetting
  • Corporate due diligence
  • Governance and board advisory
  • Compliance reviews
  • Negotiation support
Lead Partner Amarjit Singh Bedi
