II. Practice

Areas of practice.

Six core disciplines, supported by an integrated bench of advocates, solicitors and counsel admitted before the Supreme Court of India and the High Court of Delhi. Each practice is led by a senior member of the firm and supported by a dedicated team.

01

Corporate & M&A

We advise Indian and international clients on the full lifecycle of corporate transactions — from formation and joint venture structuring through to cross-border acquisitions, private equity investments and post-closing integration. Our practice is built on careful diligence, considered risk allocation, and a deep familiarity with the institutions that govern Indian business.

  • Mergers, acquisitions and disposals
  • Joint ventures and shareholder arrangements
  • Private equity and venture capital investments
  • Corporate restructuring and reorganisations
  • Foreign direct investment advisory
  • Corporate governance and board counsel
Lead Partner Rajiv Menon
02

Dispute Resolution

The firm's dispute practice represents corporate, institutional and individual clients in commercial litigation and constitutional matters before the High Court of Delhi, the Supreme Court of India and specialised tribunals. We are known for the quality of our written work and for arguing matters to judgement rather than settling for procedural advantage.

  • Commercial litigation
  • Constitutional and public-law matters
  • Insolvency and bankruptcy proceedings
  • White-collar and economic-offence defence
  • Company-law and SEBI proceedings
  • Judicial review and writ jurisdiction
Lead Partner Anitha John
03

Arbitration

Our arbitration practice has acted as counsel in domestic and international proceedings under the rules of SIAC, ICC, LCIA, HKIAC, and on an ad-hoc UNCITRAL basis. We also accept appointments as arbitrators in commercial matters. The team works seamlessly with foreign counsel and is comfortable conducting hearings in seats from New Delhi to Singapore.

  • International commercial arbitration
  • Investor–state and treaty arbitration
  • Domestic institutional and ad-hoc arbitrations
  • Section 9 and Section 17 interim relief
  • Enforcement and set-aside proceedings
  • Arbitrator appointments
Lead Partner Shreya Kapoor
04

Banking & Finance

We advise scheduled commercial banks, NBFCs, multilateral lenders and corporate borrowers on the full range of financing transactions — from project and infrastructure finance through to acquisition lending, syndicated facilities, debt restructurings and contentious recovery proceedings.

  • Project and infrastructure finance
  • Acquisition and leveraged finance
  • Syndicated and bilateral lending
  • Securitisation and structured products
  • Insolvency, restructuring and recovery
  • RBI, SEBI and IRDAI regulatory advisory
Lead Partner K. J. John
05

Real Estate & Infrastructure

The firm advises developers, institutional investors, lenders and government undertakings on land acquisition, large-scale developments, REITs and concession arrangements. We work on matters across Indian markets and have particular experience in Delhi-NCR, Mumbai and the southern metros.

  • Land acquisition and title
  • Real-estate investment trusts (REITs)
  • Concession and PPP arrangements
  • Construction and EPC contracts
  • Township and large-format developments
  • Real-estate dispute resolution
Lead Partner Rajiv Menon
06

Taxation

Our tax practice combines advisory and contentious work — counselling clients on transaction structuring, transfer pricing and indirect taxes, and representing them in disputes before tax authorities, the Income Tax Appellate Tribunal, the High Courts and the Supreme Court.

  • Direct and indirect tax advisory
  • Transfer pricing
  • GST and customs
  • International tax structuring
  • Tax dispute resolution and appeals
  • Advance rulings and APA matters
Lead Partner K. J. John
