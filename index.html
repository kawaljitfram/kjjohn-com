Three decades
at the bar.

An independent partnership, deliberately scaled, committed to the institutions and clients we serve.

Founded in 1993 by K. J. John, the firm has, over three decades, advised on matters that have shaped Indian commercial law — from the early years of liberalisation to the modern regulatory state. We act for Indian and multinational corporations, banks, regulators and individuals before the Supreme Court of India, the High Courts and the country's principal tribunals.

Our work is led personally by partners and grounded in careful preparation. The firm's recent matters have included representation in SEBI proceedings of national importance, insolvency proceedings before the NCLT and NCLAT, and arbitrations conducted under domestic and international rules.

The practice of law is, finally, a matter of judgement — and judgement is the slow accumulation of seriousness over time. K. J. John  ·  Founder
