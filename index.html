III. The Firm

Three decades
at the bar.

An independent partnership, deliberately scaled, deeply committed to the institutions and clients we serve.

Founded in 1993 by K. J. John, the firm has, over three decades, advised on matters that have shaped Indian commercial law — from the early years of liberalisation to the modern regulatory state. Our partnership remains small by design: senior lawyers lead every engagement, and our reputation rests on the quality of the counsel we provide rather than the size of the office that delivers it.

We act for Indian conglomerates, foreign investors, banks, public-sector undertakings and individuals before courts, tribunals and arbitral fora across the country. Our work is characterised by considered judgement, careful preparation and an institutional memory built across thousands of mandates.

The practice of law is, finally, a matter of judgement — and judgement is the slow accumulation of seriousness over time. K. J. John  ·  Founding Partner
