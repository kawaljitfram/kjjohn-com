New enquiries

The firm accepts new instructions in matters falling within our practice areas — commercial litigation, arbitration, insolvency, securities and regulatory matters, real estate, and corporate advisory. We do not advertise or solicit work; new mandates typically reach us through referrals from existing clients, counsel and the bar.

Conflicts are checked at intake. For matters where time is of the essence — interim relief, listing deadlines, board meetings — please mark the email accordingly.

For counsel and instructing solicitors

Briefs for opinions, drafting and court appearances may be addressed to the relevant lead partner. The chambers will allocate within the firm.

Press & speaking

Media enquiries and speaking-engagement requests for any of the partners should be sent to mail@kjjohn.com, marked for the attention of the chambers.

Recruitment

The firm hires sparingly. Applications from advocates with three or more years at the bar are reviewed on a rolling basis.
