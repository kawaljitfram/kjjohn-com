III. Approach

On the partnership.

Senior counsel personally lead engagements. The work product reflects it.

We have, by choice, remained a small partnership. Each partner accepts only the matters they can personally see through; each associate works directly with a partner on every brief. The result is a firm in which the standard of work is unusually consistent — and in which clients deal, throughout the life of a matter, with the same people.

Many of our associates have been with the firm since their first year at the bar. Several of our Of Counsel served as partners before stepping back to senior advisory roles. The chambers' senior clerk has been here since 1995.

A small firm is not a virtue in itself. A small firm in which every matter is led by a senior lawyer — that is the virtue. Anitha John  ·  Senior Partner
